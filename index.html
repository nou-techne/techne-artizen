02 — The Premise

The recovery
of craft

Techne (Greek): the craft of making — the knowledge that brings something into being.

Technology should extend human capability, not replace it. We develop collective intelligence — groups thinking better together through shared tools, language, and practice.

03 — The Opportunity

1515 Walnut Street

Boulder, Colorado

RegenHub has operated the second floor for two years — co-working, events, community building.

Now the third floor is available. We're raising funds to acquire and build out a technology learning center and Ethereum community hub above the existing co-working space.

Engelbart's Augmentation Framework

AI tools are artifacts — powerful ones — but artifacts alone don't create capability.

We develop everything that surrounds the artifact: the human capacity, the shared language, the methodology, the training that makes the whole system work.
