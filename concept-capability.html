Skills Training
Transfer of techniques. Learn the tool, get the job. Credentials certify completion. The curriculum is fixed, the path is linear, and the relationship ends at graduation.
Success metric: placement rates.
Capability Development
Formation of practitioners. Develop judgment, not just skill. The community persists because the learning never finishes. You don't graduate — you grow into deeper contribution.
Success metric: what becomes possible.
