Concept 06
Bootstrapping
"The tool and the practice co-evolve."

Engelbart's most radical insight: use your tools to improve your tools. Each cycle of practice refines the instrument, and each refinement deepens the practice. This is the compounding engine — not linear progress, but recursive amplification.

tool → practice → better tool → deeper practice → ∞
